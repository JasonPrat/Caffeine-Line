What is Caffein Line?

Caffein Line is an online cafe created for people who want to enjoy coffees, teas, and desserts in the comfort of their homes. Currently, we are focusing our delivery area on Indonesia.


Story about the Caffein Line and Us

We were a three-man team, all of us majoring in computer science. It all started at the end of the year 2020, when the three of us have experienced many business journey and confidence enough to start a business. This ambition lead us to the idea of creating the Caffein Line.


Why Caffein Line?
